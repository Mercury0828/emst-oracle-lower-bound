Driemel et al.~\cite{DMOSW25} prove that every randomized algorithm estimating $\wt(\MST(P))$ to a
constant multiplicative factor needs $\Omega(n^{1/3})$ range-counting queries. \Cref{thm:main} matches
this up to polylogarithmic and $\eps$-dependent factors, so the range-counting query complexity of
$(1\pm\eps)$-estimating Euclidean MST weight is $n^{1/3}$ up to such factors. % [expand]
